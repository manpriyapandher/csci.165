Raw Swedes

You will need:
  • 5 tbsp of root beer
  • 4 sausages
  • 5 swedes
  • 2 chicken breasts
Instructions:
  1. Bring the root beer to the boil
  2. Unwrap the sausages
  3. Cover the swedes
  4. Divide the chicken breasts
  5. Crush the chicken breasts
  6. Pound the sausages
  7. Roll the chicken breasts
  8. Whisk the root beer
  9. Prepare the sausages
